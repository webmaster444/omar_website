Requirements

The hardest single part of building a software system is deciding what to build*

Who in your project is verifying that you are actually building the right thing? The product your users actually want.

At their most basic, requirements are the basis of any specification. All systems are defined loosely by their requirements, whether or not they are documented.

Behavioural 2

This involves explicitly specifying all the behaviours of your system exactly. As many as it takes.

We encourage deliberate use of common terms for specific entities and actions within the application (and the domain).
